## Bottom line

- **As-is today:** not auditable, and correctly so — it is a research-stage template snapshot with ADR-0001 qualification UNDECIDED (`0001:33,38`). No conformity verdict is issued; the single true nonconformity is one silent editorial defect (a mis-cited §4.4 sub-clause letter, `HARM_RISK.md:32`), alongside 14 lower-tier observations. Everything else unfilled is a **declared** deferral or an inherited organizational obligation.
- **As a foundation:** strong. The 14971 method and MDR-GSPR pre-staging are clause-faithful, internally consistent, and CI-wired; adopting this template gives a project an auditable *shape* on day one, and the placeholders it demands are cheap to fill now and near-impossible to reconstruct later (ADR-0002's own argument). The distance to auditable is a finite fill-in-plus-QMS list, not a redesign.
- **To keep it drift-free:** fix the minor citation and the two cross-artifact consistency observations, then **re-run this `mdr-audit-readiness` skill at each milestone / ADR-0001 state change** — the readiness scorecard is a repeatable gate, and the diff between runs is the trail that catches new silent drift before it reaches an assessor.
